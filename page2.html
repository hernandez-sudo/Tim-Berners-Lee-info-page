Timeline of Achievements

When What
2003 Received the Computer History Museum's Fellow Award, for his seminal contributions to the development of the World Wide Web.
2004 First recipient of Finland's Millennium Technology Prize, for inventing the World Wide Web. He was awarded one million euros.
2007 Received Golden Plate Award of the American Academy of Achievement.
2009 Elected a foreign associate of the United States National Academy of Sciences.
2011 One of the first three recipients of the Mikhail Gorbachev award for "The Man Who Changed the World".
2012 Recognised for the invention of the World Wide Web in the 2012 Summer Olympics opening ceremony.
2014 Honorary Doctor of Engineering and Technology degree, Yale University.
2015 Received Gottlieb Duttweiler Prize in Zurich, Switzerland
